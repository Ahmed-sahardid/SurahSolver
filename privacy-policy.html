Privacy Policy for Surah Solver

Last updated: April 2025

What we collect

  • Location — only to fetch accurate prayer times.
  • Timer preference — stored locally in your browser for persistence.

How we use it

We only use your data in-browser; we never share it except to call the prayer-time APIs (api.aladhan.com, nominatim.openstreetmap.org).

No other data

We do not collect or store any personal details, browsing history, or communications.
